🌾 Welcome to Valley Farm!

Build a thriving colony from scratch. Farm the land, gather resources, construct buildings, and battle enemies — alone or with friends.

📖 This is a quick tutorial. Read each page to unlock the next one — it only takes 30 seconds!
🎮 Movement
  • W A S D move
  • E or Space interact
  • Camera follows you
  • You start near your house
🔢 Tool Hotbar
  • 1 Hoe   2 Watering Can
  • 3 Seeds  4 Scythe
  • 5 Hammer  6 Axe
  • 7 Pickaxe  8 Sword

🌱 Farming & Gathering

🌱 Farming (4 steps)
  • 1 Hoe → press E on grass to till
  • 2 Water → press E on tilled tile
  • 3 Seeds → press E to plant
  • 4 Scythe → press E on ripe crop
  • [ ] cycle seed types
  • Sleep to advance days & grow crops
🪓 Gathering
  • 6 Axe + E on trees → wood
  • 7 Pickaxe + E on rocks → stone
  • G forage mushrooms & berries
  • F fish on beach tiles
  • Trees & rocks regrow over time!
😴 Press F near your house to sleep — this heals you, advances the day, and grows your crops!

🏗️ Building & Crafting

🏗️ Placing Buildings
  • B open the Build menu
  • Select a building from the menu
  • Equip 5 Hammer
  • Walk to a clear tile & press E
  • Buildings give passive resources every few seconds
🔧 Crafting
  • C open the Craft menu
  • Upgrade tools (Hoe, Sword, Axe…)
  • Higher tiers = faster & stronger
  • Build a Forge to reduce craft costs by 25%
  • P buy seeds from the shop
💡 Build a Market to boost sell prices +20% and a Barracks to increase sword damage!

⚔️ Combat & Multiplayer

⚔️ Combat
  • Equip 8 Sword, press E to attack
  • 🔴 Red portal near home → Dueling Arena
  • 🟣 Purple portal → Gold Mine
  • Survive 5 waves in the arena for big rewards
  • Upgrade your sword tier to deal more damage
🌐 Multiplayer
  • Click 🌐 Multiplayer to host or join a room
  • Walk to another player's building & press E
  • Challenge them to a PvP duel in the arena
  • Winner takes their building or 500 gold!
  • Enter to chat with other players
🏆 Complete daily quests (top-left) for bonus rewards, and check your stats with O!
